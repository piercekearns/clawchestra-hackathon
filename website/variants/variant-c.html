How it works

01

Install & connect

Download the native desktop app. Point it at your project directories. Clawchestra discovers your repos and sets up managed terminal sessions.

02

Assign agents

Spin up Claude Code, Codex, or any CLI agent in embedded terminals. Route tasks from your kanban board directly to the right agent.

03

Orchestrate & ship

Monitor real-time activity, review diffs, and manage your roadmap — all from one window. The agents work. You orchestrate.
